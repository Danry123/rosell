BUKIDNON
Highland Paradise in the Heart of Mindanao

Bukidnon "means" Highlander or mountain Dweller,bukidnon also known as food basket of the region a major distributor and produce of rice,pineapple,corn,bananas and sugarcane. The situated in bukidnon is mount dulang dulangthe 2nd highest mountin in the phillipines and mount kitanglad and mount kalatungan.

Located in the centre of Mindanao,the province of Bukidnon is a place of stunning natural beauty.Surrounded by captivating Mountains,Valleys,and rivers.this landlocked paradise attracts nature lovers and adventure seekers with its breathtaking scenery.
